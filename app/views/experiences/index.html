Coping with violence in our communities

One of the causes of Mental Health amongst adolescents can tie back to the violence they experience in their communities. School being where adolescents spend most of their day, providing help and support can be beneficial in the long run. The U.S. Surgeon General estimates that 20% of American children have a diagnosable mental disorder, and only 20% of that group receives treatment. (1999) When mental health services are sited in schools, children are much more likely to receive the care they need.

How can we begin to provide support for coping with violence within our communities? This website provides an interactive way to Share your experience and begin to gain support needed.
